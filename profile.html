Chrissy and Casper

7 years, Male, Medium, Golder Retriever
Hi I am Chrissy and this is my best friend Casper.He’s incredibly smart but so terribly anxious.While he’s terrified of the broom, plastic bags, the vacuum, construction equipment, and so on, he guards this house and us with his entire heart.he’s impatient if he can’t find his toys. He howls when he doesn’t get his way. But he’s also playful. Put a puppy in the room, and Casper will play until he drops. He jumps, leaps, spins, and flings whatever plaything he can find.
